About InsightFlow AI

InsightFlow AI is a product built by a team of engineers and product designers focused on turning customer feedback into actionable business insights. Our AI-powered chatbot specializes in post-purchase conversations, helping ecommerce brands capture honest reviews, measure sentiment, and identify areas for product and service improvements.

Key philosophies:

  • User-centric conversations that respect the customer journey.
  • Non-intrusive, opt-in feedback collection with clear value to the customer.
  • Transparent analytics that translate feedback into measurable actions.
